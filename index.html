Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten curriculum includes social and academic skills including reading, math, science, social studies, art, music, and physical education.

Elementary School

Reading for information is emphasized as students build the skills to research subjects in depth. Students focus on note taking, highlighting and summarizing

Middle (10-16) Years

Along with a challenging interdisciplinary humanities course, each year of middle school includes mathematics, science, languages, arts and physical education
